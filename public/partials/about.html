About

is a simple todo app that I built to practice my skills on the MEAN stack. The app is built with AngularJS, Bootstrap and Sass on the frontend, and Node, Express, MongoDB and Mongoose on the backend. I used Passport and JSON Web Tokens to implement authentication and session management. The app is deployed on Heroku.

This app has many standard todo featuers: you can add and remove tasks, assign due dates, and view your progress with a glance. The app is also fully responsive. It serves as a good starting point for a more robust todo project sometime in the future.
